# Part B -- Prevention Checklist

## Stay Safe Online Checklist

### General Safety Tips

-   Do not share personal information like OTPs, passwords, or bank
    details\
-   Always verify unknown links before clicking\
-   Use strong and unique passwords for different accounts\
-   Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible\
-   Avoid using public Wi-Fi for sensitive transactions\
-   Keep your devices and apps updated regularly

### UPI / Financial Safety Tips

-   Never approve unknown UPI collect requests\
-   Double-check the receiver's name before sending money\
-   Do not scan QR codes sent by unknown people\
-   Never share OTP or PIN with anyone

### Report Cybercrime

-   National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in\
-   Helpline Number: 1930
