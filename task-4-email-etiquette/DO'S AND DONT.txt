📱 Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist
✅ Do’s
* Maintain a professional and respectful tone in your posts
* Think before posting and ensure your content is appropriate
* Use strong privacy settings to protect your personal information
* Share useful, educational, or positive content
* Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation


❌ Don’ts
* Do not share personal or sensitive information publicly
* Avoid posting offensive, inappropriate, or harmful content
* Do not engage in online arguments or negative discussions
* Avoid using fake profiles or misleading information
* Do not click or share suspicious links or unverified content
